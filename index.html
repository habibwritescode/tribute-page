Chief Obafemi Awolowo

Nationalist and Statesman

Timeline of his life:

  • 1909 - Born in Ikenne, Ogun State, Nigeria
  • 1919 - his father dies when he is about ten years old
  • 1927 - finished his education at Wesley College, Ibadan then heads to University of London
  • 1944 - goes to the UK to study laugh
  • 1946 - called to the Bar by the Honorable Society of the Inner Temple
  • 1947 - Path to Nigerian Freedom which was the first systematic federalist manifesto by a Nigerian politician
  • 1954 to 1960 - Premier of Western Nigeria
  • 1963 - he was imprisoned under the accusations of sedition and was not pardone until 1966
  • 1967 to 1971Federal Commissioner of Finance
  • 1987 - dies peacefully at his ikenne residence, at the age of 78
